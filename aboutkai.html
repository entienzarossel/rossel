LUX MUNDIS CUP 2024

The Lux Mundis Cup 2024, themed "Majestic Fusion," is a five-day event featuring a mix of academic, sports, and special activities. Its goal is to promote educational growth, encourage knowledge-sharing, and enhance critical thinking, while also fostering camaraderie and strengthening connections among students within the academic community.
